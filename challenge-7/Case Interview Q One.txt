Case Interview Question: Part One

50/50 Call Volume Split

You are given the following information:
    • The total number of calls per hour is 2000.
    • Calls are evenly split between sales and support (50/50 split).
    • There are two types of employees:
        ◦ Generalists who handle 10 calls per hour and are paid $10 per hour.
        ◦ Specialists who handle 25 calls per hour and are paid $20 per hour.

Question:
    • How many generalists and specialists should be employed to handle the 50/50 call split efficiently?

80 specialists should be the most efficient since they can handle both sales and support calls and they complete more calls per dollar.
